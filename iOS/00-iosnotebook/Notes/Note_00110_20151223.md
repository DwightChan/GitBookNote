* iPhone3GS, iPhone4, iPhone4S

* iPhone5, iPhone5C
* iPad4(iPad with Retina Display)

* iPhone6 Plus, iPhone6, Phone5S
* iPad Air, iPad mini2(iPad mini with Retina Display)
